Why Wolff Yoga

Who We Are: Abby and Chris Wolff

After cultivating a daily practice together beginning in 2019, we completed a 200 hr teacher training in 2021 with our teacher, Gopi Om, who considers herself a disciple of Sri Dharma Mittra. We are inspired by their teachings along with an interest in learning through sacred texts. We hope to honor the tradition of yoga and strive to live a life in accordance with the Yamas. We consider ourselves lifelong students and continue to practice and study.


Abby has worked in the field of early childhood education for over 10 years and enjoys teaching people from all walks of life. She hopes to offer classes that inspire her students to take their practice into their own hands.


Chris is a computer programmer and skateboarder and hopes to offer classes for those who are new to yoga. He has found yoga to be a compliment to not just his skateboarding practice, but to his life as well. He strives to live a life devoted to non-violence.
